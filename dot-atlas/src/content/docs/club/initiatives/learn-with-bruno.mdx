Bruno Galvão, co-founder of R0GUE is simplifying complex blockchain concepts for developers and enthusiasts alike.
Through his content series, "Learn with Bruno," he offers valuable insights and practical guidance on building within the Polkadot ecosystem.

## Who is Bruno Galvão?
Bruno, a co-founder of R0GUE and the creators of Play on Polkadot (POP), possesses extensive knowledge of Polkadot's core functionalities, including parachains, smart contracts, and cross-chain interoperability. 
He actively contributes to the blockchain community through educational YouTube content and participation in industry events and discussions.

## What is "Learn with Bruno"?
"Learn with Bruno" is an educational initiative aimed at empowering developers to build and innovate on Polkadot. The series covers a wide range of topics, from creating parachains to leveraging smart contract capabilities with ink!, Polkadot’s native language for Wasm-based smart contracts. 
Each episode delves into practical applications, making it a very good resource for newcomers and developers.

### Key episodes include:
- **[Build on Polkadot](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2FF0RS60wJc&list=PLogPKTNJxUXnSfFkvHfFhlPahlcDc3xdg&index=3)**: A step-by-step guide to developing on Polkadot.
- **[Polkadot Sessions](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=on-h9gMIMmY&list=PLogPKTNJxUXlCWUJ9hddapAuWPdtQobly&index=2)**: A series on Polkadot-related discussions.
- **[Build a Solochain](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KvISnfurA10&list=PLogPKTNJxUXn3CapB3LQ0Rj-UMfGrodsk&index=5)**: Detailed guidance on building Substrate-based solo blockchains.
- **[Polkadot UX Case Study](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ToN78BjUkco&list=PLogPKTNJxUXn9pHWUkOxHbM5q9PDqWsW1&index=1)**: Insights into user experience design within the Polkadot ecosystem.

For those looking to deepen their understanding of Polkadot or embark on their blockchain journey, Bruno’s tutorials are a good and updated resource. 
Explore the series and join the growing community of Polkadot innovators.
