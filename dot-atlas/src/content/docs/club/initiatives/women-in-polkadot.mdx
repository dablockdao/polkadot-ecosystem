Women in Polkadot is a community initiative with a focus on grow, connect, and empower the women community inside the Polkadot and wider Web3 ecosystem.

The group highlights female builders, developers, creators, and educators, and encourages collaboration, mentorship, and knowledge sharing. Through social channels and community touchpoints, it gives visibility to women led projects, showcases career paths in blockchain, and supports newcomers who want to learn and contribute.

### What Women in Polkadot focus on
* Building a supportive network for women in Polkadot and Web3  
* Sharing resources, education, and opportunities  
* Spotlighting women led projects and contributions  
* Encouraging participation in governance, development, and community roles  

### Why Women in Polkadot matters
Web3 is still largely male dominated. By creating a safe and active space for women to connect and be seen, Women of Polkadot helps make the ecosystem more diverse, resilient, and innovative.
