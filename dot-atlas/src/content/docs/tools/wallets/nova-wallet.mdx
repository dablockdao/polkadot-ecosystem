Nova Wallet is revolutionizing the mobile wallet experience by setting new standards for functionality, security, and accessibility. Designed with user-centric features, it offers robust security measures to protect your digital assets, seamless staking options to help you maximize your earnings, and extensive support for a wide range of DApps. 

With Nova Wallet, users can enjoy a streamlined and secure **gateway to the Polkadot ecosystem**, empowering them to explore, transact, and grow their digital presence.

## What is Nova Wallet?
Nova Wallet stands out as a next-generation mobile wallet designed for the Polkadot and Kusama ecosystems. With compatibility across **90+ networks** and support for **over 410 cross-chain transfer options**, it provides unparalleled accessibility to users. Whether you are managing assets, staking tokens, or interacting with decentralized applications (DApps), Nova Wallet delivers an all-in-one solution.

## Key Features of Nova Wallet:
1. **Comprehensive Asset Management**  
    - Manage assets from Polkadot, Ethereum, and other networks in one place.  
    - Send, receive, swap, and buy tokens effortlessly.  
    - Track operation history and cross-chain transfers.  
2. **Simple Staking**  
    - Stake tokens like DOT, KSM, AZERO, and GLMR directly on the blockchain.  
    - Maximize rewards with no intermediary fees.  
    - Super Simple Staking enables quick and easy staking for beginners.  
3. **Governance Participation**  
    - Access Polkadot’s OpenGov system directly from the wallet.  
    - Vote on referenda, delegate votes, and join crowdloans.  
    - A clear governance dashboard provides all relevant details.  
4. **DApp Browser & WalletConnect**  
    - Use Substrate and EVM-based DApps with the built-in browser.  
    - Connect to desktop or mobile DApps using WalletConnect.  
5. **Security & Privacy**  
    - Self-custodial wallet keeps private keys on the user’s device.  
    - Features PIN protection, phishing prevention, and hardware wallet compatibility.  
    - Open-source code available on GitHub for transparency.  

## Community and Support
Nova Wallet’s community-oriented approach is a key driver of its success. Funded by various treasuries within the ecosystem, Nova Wallet actively seeks user feedback to shape its development. The team offers comprehensive support through email, Telegram, and GitHub, ensuring users have access to help when needed.
