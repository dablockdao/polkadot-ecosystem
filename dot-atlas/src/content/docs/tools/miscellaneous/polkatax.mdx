Managing and reporting staking rewards, transfers, and trades can be a daunting task. **PolkaTax**, a comprehensive tool designed for enthusiasts and professionals in the crypto space, addresses this challenge. With its ability to summarize and export key financial data from multiple blockchain ecosystems, PolkaTax serves as a vital resource for users seeking transparency and organization in their crypto activities.

## Key Features of PolkaTax
PolkaTax stands out for its versatility and user-centric design. It caters to users across various blockchain ecosystems, including Substrate-based chains and Ethereum Layer 1 (L1) and Layer 2 (L2) networks. Below are its primary features:
1. **Staking Rewards Summarization:** PolkaTax allows users to track and summarize accumulated staking rewards. By selecting a network, fiat currency, time frame, and providing a wallet address, users can view their rewards in a graphical or tabular format. This data is exportable as CSV or JSON files, facilitating further processing or integration with other tools.
2. **Transaction and Trade History:** The platform also supports the tracking of transfers and swaps, including airdrops, liquidity pool rewards, trades, and other payments. These insights are essential for users who need detailed transaction data for tax compliance or financial analysis.
3. **Multi-Network and Multi-Currency Support:** PolkaTax supports a wide range of Substrate chains and Ethereum-based chains, including Moonbeam, Arbitrum One, Optimism, and Polygon. Additionally, it accommodates numerous fiat currencies, making it accessible to users globally.
4. **Export Options:** Users can export their data in CSV or JSON formats. This flexibility ensures compatibility with accounting software and other analysis tools.
5. **Data Visualization:** PolkaTax provides both graphical and tabular representations of staking rewards and transactions, offering a clear and intuitive interface for users.
