Gear Up for Polkadot Decoded 2024 Event in Brussels
---------------------------------------------------

This July, the heart of Europe turns into a hotspot for blockchain aficionados and innovators as Brussels, Belgium, hosts the eagerly anticipated **Polkadot Decoded 2024**. Mark your calendars for **July 11-12th** for an unparalleled blend of in-person and virtual gatherings at Polkadot’s premier international event. This year, it’s more than just a conference—it’s a festival celebrating the diverse and dynamic facets of **Polkadot** and **Kusama**.

#### Ticket Options Tailored for Every Event Attendee

Polkadot Decoded 2024 rolls out a versatile array of ticket types to cater to every participant:

- **General Admission (€149):** Enjoy full two-day access, hybrid networking, lunch vouchers, exclusive swag, and guaranteed entry to the much-anticipated opening party.
- **Basic Event Access (€49):** Get your two-day pass with hybrid networking and the chance to join the opening party, capacity permitting.
- **Student Ticket (€19):** Full event access at a discount, requiring a student email and valid ID for check-in.
- **Investor Ticket (€99):** Offers all General Admission perks plus exclusive access to the private investor lounge.
- **Virtual Admission: FREE:** Engage with the two-day event and network from wherever you are, at no cost.

You can get your tickets directly on the [**Polkadot Decoded event’s official website**](https://events.polkadot.network/event/polkadot-decoded-2024).

What to Expect of Polkadot Decoded 2024
---------------------------------------

**Polkadot Decoded 2024** promises an enriching schedule across five stages, featuring enlightening talks, panels, workshops, demos, and fireside chats. The event space will also feature a networking area, dedicated breakout rooms, and ecosystem showcase stations. These stations are specially designed for ecosystem teams and builders eager to display their technological innovations.

As an attendee, you’re set to dive deep into the potential that Polkadot holds, engage with leading minds shaping the future of Web3, and witness the groundbreaking technology underpinning Polkadot’s vision for a multichain future. This event is a treasure trove for those exploring new Web3 use cases or looking to connect with the vibrant Polkadot community.

### Can’t Join in Person? Join Virtually!

Distance won’t keep you from experiencing Polkadot Decoded 2024. The event’s live stream ensures virtual attendees can share in the excitement and innovation from any corner of the globe.

### Call for Speakers

The [Polkadot Decoded 2024 Call for Speakers is now open](https://polkadot.network/blog/polkadot-decoded-2024-call-for-speakers-now-open/). This is your golden opportunity to share your insights, technology, or live projects with thousands of community members, industry professionals, and blockchain enthusiasts. If you have a groundbreaking idea for a talk, panel, workshop, or pitch session, make sure to submit your proposal on time.

Spotlight on Dr. Gavin Wood and the Future of Polkadot
------------------------------------------------------

A highlight of Polkadot Decoded 2024 will undoubtedly be the expected **keynote speech from Dr. Gavin Wood**, co-founder of Polkadot and a pivotal figure in the blockchain world. Dr. Wood’s insights into the **future of Polkadot** are highly anticipated, particularly his discussion on **JAM.**

### In Summary

Polkadot Decoded 2024 is not just an event; it’s a celebration of innovation, collaboration, and the future of Web3. Whether you’re attending in person or virtually, get ready for an immersive experience that promises to enrich, educate, and inspire. See you in Brussels!
