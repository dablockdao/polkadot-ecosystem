Web3 Summit Speakers and Thought Leaders
The summit features an impressive lineup of speakers, continually updated, bringing together some of the brightest minds in blockchain technology and the broader decentralized web. Notable speakers include:

- Gavin Wood (Polkadot): A pioneer in blockchain technology and a key figure behind Polkadot.
- Jaron Lanier (Microsoft): A visionary in digital technology and virtual reality.
- Juan Benet (Protocol Labs): The founder of Protocol Labs, known for projects like IPFS and Filecoin.
- Sergey Nazarov (Chainlink): Co-founder of Chainlink, a decentralized oracle network.
